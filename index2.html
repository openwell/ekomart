Who we are:

Ekosmart Solutions and Engineering Limited (“Ekosmart”) is a leading independent power solutions provider in Nigeria.

Our power services emphasize optimal, clean and affordable energy solutions that meet the commercial and technical requirements of our clients and boost the overall off-grid energy capacity in Nigeria.

Our Vision:

Our vision is to be Nigeria’s foremost provider of clean and efficient comprehensive power solutions.

Our Services

Power Generation

Ekosmart generates electric power from our gas-fired plants located in Victoria Island, Lagos. Our model ensures our clients have 24/7 power with a near zero downtime. Focusing on cleaner, efficient and locally sourced energy sources such as CNG, we able to provide our clients with steady power supply unaffected by the variables of the oil market.

Power Distribution

Our dedicated distribution network spans 2000 meters covering significant areas in Oniru, Victoria Island. Our network is constantly upgrading and expanding to cover more areas in Victoria Island and around Lagos. Our dedicated network eradicates any technical losses over transmission by being directly connected to our generation plants and located around our customer service area.

Clean Energy Solutions

Our power solutions allow businesses and residences in our service areas to phase out of unclean modes of distributed generation such as diesel generation toward cleaner and quieter solutions.
